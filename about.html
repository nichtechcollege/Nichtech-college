ABOUT NICHTECH

Nichtech Technologies Training College

Nichtech Technologies Training College is a TEVETA accredited institution located in Choma, Adastra Area near Mize Lodge. The institution is committed to delivering practical, career-focused and industry-relevant training programs.

Who We Are

We provide professional technical and vocational education designed to equip students with practical skills, innovation, entrepreneurship knowledge and workplace competence.


Our Mission

To empower students with quality technical and vocational skills that meet modern industry standards and create opportunities for employment and self-reliance.


Our Vision

To become a leading centre of excellence in technical and vocational education, producing highly skilled graduates capable of transforming communities and industries.
